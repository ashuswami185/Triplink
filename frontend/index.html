Frequently Asked Questions

How do the memberships work?

+

Our membership plans give you access to book trips with influencers, along with varying levels of discounts and exclusive benefits. The membership fee is separate from the actual trip costs.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

+

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. Monthly memberships can be canceled before the next billing cycle. Annual memberships are non-refundable but will remain active until the end of the term.

Are trip costs included in the membership?

+

No, the membership gives you access to book trips and provides discounts on the trip costs. The actual trip prices are separate and vary based on destination, duration, and included amenities.

What happens if an influencer cancels a trip?

+

In the rare event that an influencer needs to cancel a trip, we offer you the option to join another similar trip, reschedule for a later date, or receive a full refund for your trip payment.
